4. **Manufacturing**: This event involves manufacturing and assembling the goods, including quality control and inspection ¹².
5. **Warehousing**: This event involves storing the finished products in a warehouse ¹.
6. **Inventory Management**: This event involves managing inventory levels, including stock replenishment and stocktaking ³.
